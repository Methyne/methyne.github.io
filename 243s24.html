Week of 3/4-3/8: Partial derivatives, tangent planes, linear approximations.

Week of 3/11-3/15: Chains rule, do you got the chainz? Directional derivatives and the gradient. To Tuesday class: the shortcut for normal vector is incorrect. If u, v are parallel, it doesn't mean that the derivatives of u and v are parallel. This is because in the equation u = cv, c can vary with time. Week of 4/1-4/5: Lagrange Lagrange Lagrange. Double integrals over rectangles, then general regions.

Week of 4/8-4/12: Polar coordinates and triple integrals. Discussion quiz 2.

Week of 4/15-4/19: Cylindrical and spherical coordinates

Week of 4/22-4/26: Vector fields, line integrals.
